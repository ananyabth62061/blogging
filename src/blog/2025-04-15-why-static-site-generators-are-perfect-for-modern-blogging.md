In the era of modern web development, **Static Site Generators (SSGs)** like Eleventy, Hugo, and Jekyll are quickly becoming the go-to tools for bloggers and developers alike.

Unlike traditional CMS platforms that rely on databases and server-side rendering, SSGs generate pre-built HTML files at build time. This approach comes with **major benefits**:

### ⚡ Blazing Fast Performance

Since the pages are static HTML, they load instantly — no database queries or server processing required. This means faster load times and better SEO.

### 🛠 Simple, Git-Based Workflow

With Netlify CMS integrated, you can manage your content through a nice UI, and your posts are stored in Git. Every change is version-controlled and transparent.

### 🔒 Secure by Default

No backend server means fewer security risks — no database vulnerabilities or plugin exploits. You deploy static files. That’s it.

### 💡 Developer-Friendly

You can use your favorite tools (Markdown, templates, custom components) and even automate builds with CI/CD.

- - -

Whether you're a solo blogger or a developer building a documentation site, static site generators give you **speed, control, and peace of mind**. Try one today — and see the difference!
